## PRICING NOTES

### Service Charge & Tax
- Service Charge: 10%
- PPN (VAT): 11%
- Total additional: 21% of subtotal

### Example Calculation:
```
Rendang Wagyu: Rp 275.000
Service (10%): Rp 27.500
PPN (11%): Rp 30.250
------------------
Total: Rp 332.750
```

### Discounts & Promotions
- VIP Member: 5% discount
- Birthday: Free dessert
- Group (>10 pax): 10% discount
